Lasagne

Description

A warm and soothing dish made out of a meat sauce, bechamel, parmesan and pasta sheets

Great choice for a family lunch

Needs to be made a day in advance

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Parmesan

Recipe steps

  1. Soak the pasta sheets
  2. Lay down the Bechamel
  3. Lay down the Bolognese
  4. Grate a hefty amount of Parmesan
  5. Lay down the pasta sheets without overlapping
  6. Repeat the steps 2-5 till the tray is full, then repeat 2-4 for the last time
  7. Bake for 45 min at 160 degrees Celsius
  8. Cool it down overnight and portion the next day
